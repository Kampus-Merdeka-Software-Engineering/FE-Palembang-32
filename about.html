About Our Website

One of the main goals of the SDGs is to reduce the Maternal Mortality Rate (MMR) and Infant Mortality Rate (IMR). The government has an important role in achieving this goal through innovation, funding and government policies. Currently, MMR is still high, at around 305 per 100,000 live births, far from the target of 183 per 100,000 live births in 2024. The role of the family is also crucial in reducing MMR and IMR, with a family approach that involves instruments, communication and community involvement. The Ministry of Health strives to provide access to quality health services, including services for pregnant women, birth assistance, post-natal care, and family planning services. Therefore, we provide online parenting services and consultations by experts to help mothers prevent children's growth and development disorders.
